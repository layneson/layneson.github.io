French Toast

Ingredients

  • 6 slices thick bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • just shy of 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • pinch of cloves
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp bourbon (optional)

Directions

  1. Beat together all ingredients (except bread).

  2. Heat a lightly buttered skillet over medium-high heat.

  3. Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides. Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden. Serve hot.
