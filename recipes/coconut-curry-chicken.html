Coconut Curry Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon curry
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 pound cubed chicken breast (1-inch pieces)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (from can)
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons coconut flakes (optional garnish)

Directions

  1. Combine curry and salt in bowl. Add chicken and coat.

  2. Heat some oil in pan. Cook onion, garlic, and ginger for 1 minute. Add coated chicken to pan and sear 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add coconut milk and lime juice and continue cooking on low to medium heat. Stir occasionally. Cook 15 minutes.

  4. (Optional) Toast coconut flakes until lightly browned. Serve on top of chicken.
