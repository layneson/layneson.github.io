Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • t tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Directions

  1. Sift dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar) into bowl.

  2. Add butter, egg, and milk. Immediately whisk vigorously until combined. Let sit for 5 minutes.

  3. Preheat skillet, grease with oil. Use 1/4 cup measuring spoon to portion. Turn pancakes when bubbles come through middle.
