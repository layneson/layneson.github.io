Baked Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
    • Butter for dish
    • 2 teaspoons salt
    • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • Cheese Sauce
    • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
    • 4 tablespoons flour
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 2 cups milk
    • 2 cups grated sharp cheddar (from 1 8oz block)
  • Topping
    • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
    • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Butter a 2-quart casserole dish.
  2. Bring 4 quarts of water to boil. Add salt and macaroni. Cook until tender, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Meanwhile, melt butter in pan. Whisk in flour and salt, stirring over medium heat until the roux bubbles and the flour turns pale brown. Slowly whisk in the milk. Continue to stir constantly until the sauce thickens. Add the shredded cheese and mix until melted.
  4. Mix macaroni with sauce. Fill the prepared dish. Mix breadcrumbs with melted butter and spread on top.
  5. Bake until the topping is browned and the dish bubbles around the edges, about 15-20 minutes.
